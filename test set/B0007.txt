<h1>Statement Of Cover (Sample Document)</h1>
<p><b><span class="highlighti">**SAMPLE**SAMPLE**SAMPLE**SAMPLE**SAMPLE**SAMPLE**</span></b></p>
<p>Please read this Statement of Cover now and make sure you understand your cover.
It has been arranged in accordance with your requirements and on the basis of information
provided by you, details of which may be supplied on request.</p>
<p>If you decide that you do not want this insurance, please return the Statement
of Cover to the Cheshire Building Society, Head Office, Castle Street, Macclesfield,
Cheshire SK11 6AF within 28 days of commencement of cover.  No charge for cover will
apply providing no claim has been made.</p>
<p>If your circumstances or requirements change please make sure you notify the Society,
for example if:</p>
<ul>
<li><p>anything happens to change the use or nature of your home;</p>
<li><p>you leave your Home unoccupied for longer than 60 consecutive days - the Society
will let you know if any special conditions will apply to your insurance while you
are away;</p>
<li><p>you do anything which may affect its rebuilding cost - for example, extending
the property, installing central heating or double glazing, or <b>You </b>add to the
number of bedrooms to <b>Your Home.</b></p>
</ul>
<p>We wish to give our customers a high standard of service at all times. If you have
any comment or query about your insurance please contact <a href="Statement_Of_Cover.asp#cbsaddress">the
Society</a>.</p>
<h5>24 Hour Helpline Service</h5>
<p>As a Cheshire policyholder, you and members of your household have access, free
of charge, 24 hours a day, 365 days a year, to two <a href="Statement_Of_Cover.asp#helplines">helplines</a>.</p>

<p>Naturally <b>We</b> hope <b>You</b> won’t have any accidents or misfortune, but

<ul>

to see.</p>

<b>Policy</b>.</p>

does. Remember too that the amount <b>You</b> are entitled to claim may be reduced
if <b>Your</b> property has not been properly maintained.</p>
<li><p>Please do not dispose of damaged items before <b>We</b> have had the opportunity
to inspect them.</p>
<li><p>Report any incident involving theft, malicious damage or hit and run damage
to the Police.</p>
<li><p>If someone is making a claim against <b>You</b> for any injury to them or damage
to their property, <b>You</b> must send <b>Us</b> full details, in writing, as soon
as possible. Any letters or documents <b>You</b> receive should be sent to <b>Us</b>,
unanswered, without delay. It is most important that <b>You</b> leave <b>Us</b> to
deal with the matter on <b>Your</b> behalf and do not get involved in any correspondence
or conversation with the other person.</p>
<li><p>Depending on the type of claim and value involved <b>We</b> may:</p>
<ul>
<li><p>Contact <b>You</b> to progress <b>Your</b> claim</p>
<li><p>Arrange for one of <b>Our</b> nation-wide team of Claims Advisers to personally

<li><p>Appoint an independent loss adjuster or representative to deal with <b>Your</b>
claim on <b>Our</b> behalf</p>
</ul>
<li><p><b>Our</b> aim is to deal with <b>Your</b> claim promptly, efficiently and
