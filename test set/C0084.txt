<p><font color="#FF0000"><strong>Part A Buildings</strong></font> </p>

<p>In addition to your house, outbuildings, greenhouses, conservatory, swimming pool,
patios, hard tennis courts, driveways, footpaths, terraces, walls, gates, hedges and
fences being protected, you are also covered for:- </p>

<p>Fire, storm, flood, collision, vandalism and subsidence. You are also covered for
underground cables, pipes and accidental breakage to fixed glass, double glazing and
sanitary ware; </p>

<p>Loss of rent, architect and surveyors fees are covered up to 20% of the buildings sum
insured; </p>

<p>Outdoor items; </p>

<p>Property owner&#146;s liability and defective premises liability are covered for up to
£2,000,000; </p>

<p>The sum insured for buildings will be automatically adjusted upwards to keep pace with
inflation. </p>

<p>Accidental damage can be included for an additional £25.00 </p>

<p>You are not covered for the following which are some of the main exclusions: </p>

<p>The deductible of £100 unless the claim exceeds £10,000 (when no deductible applies
except for subsidence claims where the deductible is always £1000); </p>

<p>Accidental damage when the Buildings are commercially let to a third party (other than
relatives); </p>

<p>Outdoor items not covered against storm or flood. </p>

<p><font color="#FF0000"><strong>Part B Contents</strong></font> </p>

<p>This part of the policy covers all your household goods and personal property,
furniture and furnishings. Accidental damage is included at no additional cost. Coverage
includes fire, flood, theft, burglary, collision, vandalism, audio equipment and
televisions, mirrors and freezer contents. Contents are also covered when temporarily
removed from the premises anywhere in the world, as well as: </p>

<p>Loss of rent and alternative accommodation are covered for up to 20% of your contents
and fine art sums insured; </p>

<p>Replacement of locks are covered for up to £1,000; </p>

<p>Liability as occupier and legal liability are covered for up to £2,000,000; </p>

<p>The sum insured for contents will be adjusted upwards to keep pace with inflation. You
only need list those items in excess of £15,000. </p>

<p>You are not covered for the following which are some of the main exclusions: </p>

<p>The deductible of £100 unless the claim exceeds £10,000 (when no deductible applies)</p>

<p>Accidental damage or theft when the premises is commercially let to a third party
(other than relatives); </p>

<p>The most insurers will pay for items of gold, silver, gold and silver plate, jewellery
and pearls is £5,000 unless such items, pair or set are individually insured under the
more relevant sections of the policy (part C &amp; D). </p>

<p>The excess can be reduced to £50 for an additional £75.00 </p>

<p><font color="#FF0000"><strong>Part C Fine Art </strong></font></p>

<p>You are covered for fine art, antiques, objet d&#146; art and items of gold, silver,
gold and silver plate and other precious metals. </p>

<p>This attractive cover includes accidental damage or breakage covering physical loss or
damage, restoration and any resulting depreciation in value following and insured peril
whilst in your home or anywhere in the world. </p>

<p>We can also provide you with automatic coverage for new purchases you acquire anywhere
in the world for up to 15% of your sum insured and giving you 60 days to notify Insurers. </p>

<p>In the event of a claim, our unique inflation protection clause enables works of art to
increase in value by up to 200% of the original declared value when your policy incepted
to take account of a rise in value that may have resulted since the last valuation. (For
example the death of an artist). This is useful in respect of loss or damage to a single
item, pair or set, however please note that the most that the insurer will pay is the
total sum insured declared on the proposal form and schedule. </p>

<p>You only need to list those items, pairs or sets with a value in excess of £15000. </p>

<p>You are not covered for the following which is one of the main exclusions: </p>

<p>Loss or damage from unattended vehicles. </p>

<p><font color="#FF0000"><strong>Part D Personal Possessions</strong></font> </p>

<p>You are covered for personal possessions including jewellery, furs, money, credit cards
and pedal cycles. This extremely broad cover includes accidental damage and any physical
loss or damage whilst anywhere in the world. </p>

<p>We can also provide you with automatic coverage for new purchases you acquire anywhere
in the world for up to 15% of your sum insured and giving you 60 days to notify insurers. </p>

<p>You only need list those items, pairs or sets with a value in excess of £5,000. </p>

<p>The maximum sum insured for money is £250 </p>

<p>The maximum sum insured for credit cards is £500 </p>

<p>You are not covered for the following which are some of the main exclusions: </p>

<p>The deductible of £100 unless the claim exceeds £10,000 (when no deductible applies);
</p>

<p>Damage to sports equipment whilst in use; </p>

<p>Loss of jewellery from your baggage unless such baggage was under your supervision and
carried by you; </p>

<p>Loss or damage to pedal cycles which are not securely locked when they are left
unattended. </p>
