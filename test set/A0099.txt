      <p>You'll need to insure the cost of replacing everything in your home as 
        new. </p>
      <p>This calculator will help you work out the amount of your contents sum 
        insured. All you have to do is fill in the amount it would cost to buy 
        the same items new today.</p>
      <p>Some items are more at risk of being stolen than others. We've listed 
        these as high risk items. They have a limit of 1/3 of your contents sum 
        insured in total and £2,000 for a single item. If your high risk items 
        are worth more than this, please tell us so that we can make sure you're 
        covered properly.</p>
